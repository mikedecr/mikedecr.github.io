About

UPDATED DEC 4, 2017

I am a doctoral student in the Department of Political Science at the University of Wisconsin–Madison. I study American politics and statistical methodology.

My research focuses on electoral politics, broadly construed (representation, voting, public opinion, and election administration) and Bayesian data analysis. My dissertation explores primary elections and the representation of local constituency preferences.

I am an affiliate of the Elections Research Center, where I also serve as the graduate student assistant. I am also the co-coordinator of the Political Behavior Research Group at UW–Madison.
