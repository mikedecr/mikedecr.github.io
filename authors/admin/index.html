Michael DeCrescenzo

Ph.D. Candidate, Political Science

University of Wisconsin–Madison

I am a doctoral student at the University of Wisconsin–Madison, where I develop and apply new Bayesian approaches to the study of U.S. elections, election administration, public opinion, and representation.

I am an affiliate of the Elections Research Center and an Honored Instructor at UW–Madison.

I expect to finish my Ph.D. and begin a non-academic career in 2020. You may view my CV here.
